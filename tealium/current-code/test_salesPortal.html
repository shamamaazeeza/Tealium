Hello from Anne Altman (Rich text)

Anne Altman, General Manager, US Federal Government

We are in the midst of an exciting transformation in government IT. IBM's robust portfolio of products and services can help you manage the technology shifts underway and prepare for the future.

This portal is designed to provide you with information on the key areas driving innovation in government IT services, including cloud computing, big data and analytics, mobility, social media and security. It also serves as a point of entry for IBM solutions, including details on contract information and a point of contact for your agency. I hope this and the related content will help you as you work to best serve our country and its citizens.
